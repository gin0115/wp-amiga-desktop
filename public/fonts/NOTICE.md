# Topaz font

`topaz.woff2` and `topaz.woff` are copies of `AmigaTopazTurkish.woff2` and `AmigaTopazTurkish.woff` from:

https://github.com/emartisoft/AmigaTopazFont

That repository does not declare an explicit licence. The font is bundled here on a use-at-your-own-risk basis to recreate the Workbench 3.1 visual style for personal and educational purposes. If the original author or any rights holder objects, the files will be removed promptly — open an issue.

The "Topaz" name and bitmap design originate from the Commodore Amiga system font shipped with AmigaOS.
